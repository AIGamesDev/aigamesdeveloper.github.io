WHAT IS ERA 3?

Era 3 is the next period in my Scratch career when it comes to making projects. Project quality will increase significantly, showing that it's better than the previous Era, Era 2. Previously called Generations, these would usually be different time periods for projects. Eras are usually separated by quality of project, which is how I originally thought of them in the first place. Era 1 lasted from August 28, 2020 to May 4th, 2021. Era 2 lasted from May 5th, 2021 to June 10th, 2024. Era 3 started on September 25th, 2024, with no confirmed end date. Currently, it's looking like an end date of 2025-2026, but it all depends.

PROJECTS FOR ALL

When making projects,I have to consider my community and what they want, based on what they have liked. Era 3 will have projects that a majority of people will enjoy hopefully. All types of devices will be considered, whether you are on a computer, or on a touchscreen device. There won't be any restrictions on what you can play projects on.

BIGGER AND BETTER

Projects in Era 3 will be bigger. There will be more code included in these projects. The main reason why is so that the project runs very well with no flaws at all. I want to make sure that the project runs perfectly without any bugs/glitches occuring, ruining the experience. I will spend tons of time working on these projects in order to make them nearly flawless.

ARE YOU PREPARED?

Expect a new project from Era 3 every 2-4 months, meaning that there will be 3-6 projects a year. Currently, I have multiple projects in development right now.
